About ICNASA

The International Conference on Nanophotonics and Advanced Sensing Applications (ICNASA) is an annual international conference co-organised by RMIT University, the Centre for Atomaterials and Nanomanufacturing (CAN), and E2CROP. The conference brings together leading researchers, engineers, and industry professionals from around the world to share advances in nanophotonics, advanced manufacturing, and sensing technologies.

ICNASA has established itself as a premier venue for disseminating cutting-edge research across multiple disciplines and fostering international collaborations.
