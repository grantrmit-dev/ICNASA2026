Getting to Melbourne

✈ By Air

Melbourne Airport (Tullamarine) is the main international gateway, approximately 23 km from the city centre. The SkyBus Express operates 24/7 between the airport and Southern Cross Station.

🚂 By Train / Tram

RMIT City Campus is a 5-minute walk from Melbourne Central Station (Swanston St exit). Multiple tram routes stop directly in front of the campus on Swanston Street.

🚗 By Car

Several secure parking garages are available within walking distance of campus. Check RMIT's parking guide for current rates and locations. City Loop stations are preferred over driving.

🚃 Free Tram Zone

The Melbourne CBD is covered by a Free Tram Zone — you can ride any tram within the zone at no cost. RMIT University is inside the Free Tram Zone. Simply board and alight anywhere in the zone without needing a ticket.

Visa Information

International attendees may require a visa to enter Australia. We recommend applying as early as possible. ICNASA 2026 can provide a Letter of Invitation upon request to support your visa application.

  • Check visa requirements via the Australian Department of Home Affairs
  • To request an invitation letter, contact us at icnasa@rmit.edu.au
  • Electronic Travel Authority (ETA) is available for eligible passport holders
