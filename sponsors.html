Become a Sponsor

Sponsoring ICNASA 2026 is an excellent opportunity to connect with leading researchers, academics, and industry professionals from around the world in the fields of nanophotonics and advanced sensing.

Sponsorship benefits include:

  • Logo placement on conference website, proceedings, and promotional materials
  • Exhibition booth space at the conference venue
  • Complimentary registrations (number varies by tier)
  • Speaking opportunities and workshop hosting
  • Direct access to a global network of researchers and innovators

To discuss sponsorship opportunities, contact us at icnasa@rmit.edu.au.
